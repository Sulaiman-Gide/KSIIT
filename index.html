What Will You Learn In Our Training Course

  • 1

    Optimizing your site for mobile devices

    One of the keys of great a frontend is having a mobile friendly website which works smoothly on all devices

  • 2

    Understanding user behavior on websites

    In frontend development, it's important to understand how users interact with websites, going beyond keyword optimization and content creation to create meaningful user experiences.

  • 3

    Write for humans optimize for engines

    In website development, it's essential to craft well-structured and understandable content that resonates with human readers, while also incorporating SEO strategies to enhance visibility on search engines.

  • 4

    Analyse your existing search traffic

    A good action plan comes out of understanding where your current position is and the environment.

  • 5

    Keep updated with the latest changes

    Google changes it's search indexing algorithm twice a year so it's important to stay updated with news.

  • 6

    Security Implementation

    Frontend developers play a role in implementing security measures such as HTTPS encryption, input validation, and protection against common web vulnerabilities like cross-site scripting (XSS) and SQL injection to safeguard user data and prevent unauthorized access.
